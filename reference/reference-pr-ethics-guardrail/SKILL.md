# PR Ethics Guardrail

This skill prevents reputational shortcuts in earned media, creator, testimonial, and data-story work.

## Trigger

Use before sending or approving:

- journalist pitches, media lists, press releases, founder POV notes, or data-PR reports;
- creator outreach, Hashgifted briefs, UGC briefs, or testimonial reel scripts;
- funeral, hospice, bereavement, or cause-led partnership messaging;
- any public claim about customers, order data, growth, competitors, partners, or recipient reactions.

## Hard Rules

- Real testimonials only. Never invent quotes, names, star ratings, screenshots, reactions, or DMs.
- No fake journalist, customer, partner, charity, or creator identities.
- No astroturfing, review gating, undisclosed paid placement, or undisclosed gifted product.
- Attribute evidence. Every claim needs a source, dataset, interview note, order record, public source, or explicit human approval.
- Anonymise customer/order data before external use. Remove direct identifiers and small-cell data that could reveal a person.
- Do not imply a partnership, charity endorsement, or media relationship until it is confirmed in writing.
- Sympathy and bereavement PR must be useful, restrained, and reviewed for tone before outreach.

## Evidence Ledger

Maintain a short ledger for every PR asset:

| Claim or asset | Evidence source | Permission status | Public wording approved? |
|---|---|---|---|
| [claim] | [link/file/interview/order export] | Yes / Pending / Not required | Yes / No |

If the ledger is incomplete, the pitch or asset is not ready.

## Review Workflow

1. Classify the work: media, creator, testimonial, data story, partnership, or cause-led.
2. Extract every factual claim and quote.
3. Populate the evidence ledger.
4. Check disclosure language for gifted, paid, affiliate, or partner relationships.
5. Check customer privacy and data aggregation.
6. For grief, funeral, hospice, or bereavement work, run a tone check against the brand voice guardrail.
7. Approve, approve with edits, or block.

## Output Format

```markdown
## PR Ethics Review

**Asset:** [asset]
**Type:** [media / creator / testimonial / data / partnership]
**Verdict:** Approved / Approved with edits / Blocked

### Evidence Ledger
[table]

### Required Fixes
[specific edits, missing evidence, or disclosure changes]
```

## Escalation

Escalate to the CEO when a requested PR claim cannot be evidenced, a partner/charity relationship is ambiguous, a journalist relationship creates a conflict, or data privacy risk remains after anonymisation.
