# Deputy Workflow

## What Deputy Contains

- All staff pay rates (used to calculate the $ commitment indicator)
- Staff availability settings per worker
- Planned leave
- Published rosters visible to staff via the Deputy app

## Building the Roster in Deputy

1. Open the weekly roster view
2. Start with full-timers — their standard hours are typically pre-populated or templated
3. Add part-timers — assign shifts to meet their minimum weekly hours
4. Add casuals — fill gaps and balance studio coverage
5. Watch the **$ commitment indicator** in real time as you add shifts

## Budget Check in Deputy

The dollar indicator shows total committed labour spend for the week. Compare this against your calculated labour budget (`forecast_revenue × 0.085`).

If over budget:
- Remove or shorten shifts (casuals first, then part-timers to their minimums)
- Re-check indicator after each change

## Publishing

- Once indicator is under budget and all coverage looks right: click **Publish**
- Staff receive an in-app notification in the Deputy mobile app
- No separate communication required

## Data Not in Deputy

- Gross revenue → pull from Shopify
- 7-day sales forecast → calculate manually from Shopify last-7-days data
