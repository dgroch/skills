# Roster Rules & Constraints

## Staff Types

| Type | Hours | Flexibility |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Full-time | 38h/week fixed | None — schedule as-is |
| Part-time | Variable minimum per worker | Must meet minimum; can add more |
| Casual | No guaranteed hours | Fully flexible; keep engaged |

## Budget Formula

```
labour_budget = shopify_gross_revenue_last_7_days × 0.085
```

This is a hard cap. Roster must be published under this number.

## Availability Rules

- Every worker has a set of days they're available — check before scheduling
- Planned annual leave always takes priority — remove from the week entirely
- Some staff are flagged **"no solo Saturday"** — must always have another experienced staff member rostered alongside them on Saturdays

## Cutting Shifts (when over budget)

Priority order for cuts:
1. Trim casual shifts first (reduce hours, don't eliminate entirely)
2. Trim part-timer shifts next (only down to their minimum commitment)
3. Never cut full-timers (fixed contract)

## Engagement Rule

Never zero out a casual's hours for the week if any shifts remain — keep everyone getting at least some hours to prevent attrition.

## Studios

Fig & Bloom has **three studios**. The roster must ensure adequate coverage at each location across all trading days.

## Public Holiday Rostering Policy

Fig & Bloom **trades on all Australian public holidays** (e.g. ANZAC Day, Australia Day, Melbourne Cup, etc.) at **all three studios** (SYD, MEL, BNE).

### Staffing on Public Holidays

- Roster approximately **half the usual part-time crew** at each studio.
- **Full-time workers**: receive public holiday pay via payroll — **do NOT roster them in Deputy** for the public holiday day. Their PH pay is processed outside Deputy.
- **Casual workers**: may be rostered at the manager's discretion as part of the half-crew.

### Deputy Rate Multiplier

Deputy automatically applies a pay rate multiplier for public holiday shifts (~1.25× observed). Before publishing a PH roster, note in the comments/handover that payroll should **confirm this multiplier matches the applicable Fair Work award obligations** for the relevant award classification.

### Checklist for PH weeks

1. Identify any Australian public holidays in the roster week (check by state — QLD, NSW, VIC may differ).
2. For each PH day: remove all full-time workers from Deputy shifts.
3. For each PH day: roster approximately 50% of the usual PT workers per studio.
4. Flag to payroll: confirm Deputy's PH rate multiplier matches Fair Work award rate for each worker classification.
