## Inheritance (read top-down, most specific wins)

1. **Parent Editorial Framework** — brand voice, core creative principles
   (Emotional Certainty / Craft Proof / Delivery Confidence), messaging
   hierarchy, tone guardrails, cross-channel consistency checklist. *Governs
   everything.*
2. **Month Creative Direction** (e.g. May "The Message After Matters") —
   month tone, visual world + palette, messaging pillars, **headline bank**,
   body-copy direction, photography/design direction, and the **Weekly Content
   Map** (which day is the Blog slot + its focus line). This is usually where
   the post's actual angle is specified.
3. **The post page** — may be just a title + intent line; the month doc fills in
   the rest. Don't expect a full spec on the post page itself.

## What to extract for the draft

- The **moment**: occasion, sender role, recipient emotional state, the fear of
  getting it wrong, the good outcome.
- The **messaging pillar** the post serves (e.g. May Pillar 2: "You do not need
  to be a writer").
- **Headline bank** candidates (adapt, don't copy verbatim).
- **Required example messages** (educational months mandate 2–3 generic-vs-
  specific examples).
- **Palette / visual world** (for imagery briefs handed to the photographer
  skill) and any **success markers**.
