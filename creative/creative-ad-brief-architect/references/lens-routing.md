# Lens routing — the persuasion bench

The five-step framework is the *structure*; the lenses shape *how* an ad
persuades. Route across the whole bench — not just Robbins and Ziglar. Pick or
blend the lens(es) that fit the occasion and intent, **weight them, and state the
routing** as a string, e.g. `Hormozi 45 / Godin 30 / Ziglar 25` (weights sum to
100). Put the routing in the brief header and the Ads DB row.

Full definitions and the 10 × 9 lens-by-occasion matrix live in the **Sales
Legends Playbook** (Notion). These one-liners are enough to route; open the
Playbook when you need the per-lens framework or signature move.

## The ten lenses (essence)

Round one — persuasion & motivation
- **Tony Robbins** — change the state; hand them a future memory.
- **Zig Ziglar** — relationship and sincerity; the reframe that disarms.
- **Robert Cialdini** — proof and belonging; show the room.

Round two — copy, offer, system, salesmanship, movement
- **Eugene Schwartz** — channel the desire that already exists.
- **David Ogilvy** — build a brand image; respect the reader.
- **Alex Hormozi** — engineer an irresistible offer.
- **Chet Holmes** — sell to the 97% who aren't ready today.
- **Gary Halbert** — find the starving crowd first.
- **Joe Girard** — sell yourself; the relationship is the product.
- **Seth Godin** — people buy a version of themselves.

## Occasion → lead-lens defaults (overridable)

Routing is a blend; these set the **lead** lens. Adjust weights to the specific
idea, then state the final string.

| Occasion (Ads DB) | Lead lens | Typical blend |
|---|---|---|
| Anniversary | Girard (relationship) | `Girard 40 / Robbins 35 / Ziglar 25` |
| Apology / I'm Sorry | Ziglar (sincerity) | `Ziglar 50 / Cialdini 25 / Ogilvy 25` |
| Birthday | Robbins (state / celebration) | `Robbins 45 / Godin 30 / Hormozi 25` |
| Congratulations | Robbins | `Robbins 45 / Godin 30 / Cialdini 25` |
| Get Well | Ziglar (tender, presence) | `Ziglar 50 / Cialdini 25 / Ogilvy 25` |
| Thinking of You | Ziglar / Godin | `Ziglar 40 / Godin 35 / Robbins 25` |
| I Love You | Robbins / Girard | `Robbins 40 / Girard 35 / Schwartz 25` |
| Mothers Day | Robbins / Ziglar | `Robbins 40 / Ziglar 35 / Hormozi 25` |
| New Baby | Robbins / Godin | `Robbins 40 / Godin 35 / Cialdini 25` |
| Dinner Party (Entertainer) | Godin (identity) | `Godin 45 / Ogilvy 30 / Schwartz 25` |
| Evergreen | Schwartz / Halbert | `Schwartz 40 / Halbert 30 / Hormozi 30` |

**Tender occasions** (Apology, Get Well, Condolences/Sympathy) lead **Ziglar** —
presence is the proof, the guarantee stays a quiet closing reassurance.
**Celebratory** occasions lead **Robbins**. **Anniversary** leans **Girard**.
Offer-led prospecting (first-order acquisition) raises **Hormozi**'s weight.

## Notes

- Routing changes *texture and emphasis*, never the five-step structure or the
  Whoosh test.
- Standing rule across every lens: **never generic flowers — always a real
  Fig & Bloom design.**
