# Offers — for the Proof step (risk reversal)

The canonical offers for direct-response advertising. Locked for now — test and
iterate against these before adding new ones. Mirrors the Notion Offers page;
re-pull if in doubt.

## Standing principles

- **Value, never discount.** Every offer adds value rather than cutting price —
  this protects the premium position and never trains customers to wait for a
  sale.
- **Real designs only.** Every creative features an actual Fig & Bloom design
  (Osaka, Marseille, Lucerne, Umbria, Monaco…) — never generic flowers.
- **Lead with the offer.** The florals are the visual; the offer is the message.
  Put the offer line in/near the hook, not buried on the product page.
- **Endgame is the stack.** Once a first-order winner emerges, combine it with
  the freshness promise into one irresistible first-order offer (Hormozi-style).

## Default stack for prospecting

**Free Size Upgrade behind the 7-Day Freshness Promise.** This is the proof-step
default unless the brief says otherwise.

## 1 · Always-on guarantee (sits under every ad)

**7-Day Freshness Promise** — *"Still beautiful in seven days — or we'll resend,
free."* Pure risk reversal; removes the gifter's deepest fear. The headline
guarantee beneath all advertising and the control everything is measured against.
For tender occasions, hold it as a quiet closing reassurance, not a super.

## 2 · First-order incentives (test head-to-head)

- **Free Size Upgrade** — *"Your first bloom, one size bigger — on us."* The safe,
  reliable workhorse for first-order acquisition. Abundance reads as generosity.
- **First-Order Exclusive Design** — *"One design. First order only. Not on the
  site."* Exclusivity/FOMO via access, not a lower price. Scarcity-led creative.
- **First-Order Keepsake** — *"Flowers fade. We'll send something that doesn't."*
  Candle or pressed-and-framed bloom. Emotional, identity-led; a path into the
  lifestyle range.

## 3 · Lead capture (not ready today — note as future)

- **20% More Stems for Subscribers** — *"Join the list. Every bouquet, 20% more
  stems."* Top-of-funnel lead capture; the closest to discounting — guard it.
- **Never Miss a Date Again** — *"Never miss another birthday."* Free reminder
  service; sells the "one who always remembers" identity, captures data.

## Offer → occasion fit

- Prospecting / first-time buyers → **Free Size Upgrade** (default) + 7-Day
  promise. Raise Hormozi in the lens routing.
- Scarcity / "new here" angles → First-Order Exclusive Design.
- Emotional / identity / lifestyle angles → First-Order Keepsake.
- Tender occasions → lead on presence; 7-Day promise as a quiet closing line.
