## Hero Patterns (confirmed from 50 emails)

| Pattern | % | Description | Primitives |
|---------|---|-------------|-----------|
| Text-on-Photo | 60% | Large lifestyle/studio photo with bold headline overlay + CTA | hero-a, hero-b variants |
| Photo + Text Band | 20% | Full-bleed photo top, white/clay/noir band below with headline | hero-c variants |

| Split Layout | 5% | Photo left, text right (or vice versa) | hero-c variants |
| Multi-Image Collage | 5% | Grid of 2-4 photos as hero | hero-image-only + grid |

## White Space Standards

- Between sections: 48-64px padding
- Within sections: 24-32px padding
- Around CTAs: 32px padding
- Around product cards: 16-24px
- Container: 600px max-width
- Product images: 280-350px (horizontal cards)

## Consistent Decorative Elements (all emails)

1. Floral line illustrations as section dividers
2. 5-6 service icons at bottom (Fresh Daily Flowers, Every Occasion, Australia-Wide, Same Day, Order Before 1pm)
3. Brand logo centered at top and bottom

## Theme-Specific Decorations

- **Christmas:** Gold snowflakes, stars, holly leaves
- **Valentine's:** Heart icons, pink accents, dark red hero
- **Easter:** Painted eggs, daisy icons, pink tape effects, scalloped edges
- **Giveaway:** Golden floral outlines, wavy dividers
- **Earth Day:** Green backgrounds, leaf motifs, annotations/arrows on product images
- **Product Launch (Luxury):** Monochromatic, no decorative elements, clean minimalism

## Typography System

- **Headlines:** Bold sans-serif (Neuzeit Gro), 28-36px, all caps or sentence case
- **Body Text:** Clean sans-serif Light 300, 14-16px, dark gray #333
- **Script/Accent:** Cervanttis cursive, max 3 uses/email, usually lowercase
- **Brand Name:** Serif (Lust) for "Fig & Bloom" logo
