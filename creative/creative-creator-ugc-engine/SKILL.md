# Creator UGC Engine

This skill helps Muse build a repeatable creator and testimonial pipeline that produces usable creative without fake proof or unclear usage rights.

## Trigger

Use when asked to:

- plan or run Hashgifted campaigns;
- build creator outreach sequences;
- shortlist creators;
- brief UGC content;
- produce testimonial reels;
- collect usage rights;
- hand off assets to Buzz, paid media, Echo, or Herald.

Run `reference-pr-ethics-guardrail` before outreach or publication and `reference-brand-voice-guardrail` before approving scripts/captions.

## Campaign Setup

Define:

- campaign objective: awareness, proof, paid creative, Birthday Club, Bower, corporate, sympathy;
- product or offer;
- creator profile;
- required deliverables;
- usage rights needed;
- disclosure requirements;
- timeline;
- shipping constraints;
- approval owner.

## Creator Fit Score

Score 1-5:

| Criterion | What Good Looks Like |
|---|---|
| Audience fit | Australian, relevant life stage, gifting/home/style affinity |
| Content quality | natural light, clear audio, stable framing, tasteful edits |
| Trust | real engagement, no obvious fake follower pattern |
| Brand fit | restrained, warm, not shouty or discount-led |
| Reliability | past delivery evidence or strong communication |
| Rights fit | willing to grant needed usage rights |

Shortlist creators scoring 24+ unless there is a documented reason.

## Outreach Sequence

1. Initial invite: specific reason, product/program, deliverables, disclosure, timeline.
2. Follow-up after 3-5 business days.
3. Confirmation: shipping, brief, due dates, usage rights.
4. Reminder before delivery and before content due date.
5. Thank-you and asset collection.

Never pressure creators to give a positive review. Ask for honest content and only use testimonials that are real and permissioned.

## Testimonial Reel Template

Structure:

1. Problem or sender context.
2. Product/gesture arriving.
3. Detail or care proof.
4. Real reaction, message-back, or testimonial quote.
5. Quiet CTA.

Rules:

- Use real words from the customer/creator.
- Keep quote source and permission status in the evidence ledger.
- Avoid scripted fake reactions.
- Keep captions in Australian English and remove exclamation marks.

## Quality Gates

- Creator disclosure and usage rights are explicit.
- Real testimonials only.
- No fabricated quotes, fake DMs, or staged customer claims.
- Gifted or paid relationships are disclosed.
- Assets are labelled by campaign, rights, expiry, and approved channels.
