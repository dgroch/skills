# Bower Launch Control

This skill helps Bower Ops launch and scale Bower without contaminating Fig & Bloom positioning or spending past payback discipline.

## Trigger

Use when work involves:

- Bower launch plans;
- Bower value bundles or offers;
- Bower paid media tests;
- Bower creative/copy QA;
- Bower CAC, margin, or payback reporting;
- shared assets, audiences, or operations between Bower and Fig & Bloom.

Always run `reference-brand-separation-guardrail` and `reference-spend-discipline-guardrail`.

## Launch Control Checklist

| Area | Required Decision |
|---|---|
| Brand promise | What Bower promises that Fig & Bloom does not |
| Offer | Bundle, price, threshold, expiry, and margin |
| Creative | Asset source, logo, colour, tone, product proof |
| Channel | Organic, paid, email, PR, partner, creator |
| Measurement | CAC, contribution margin, payback, AOV, repeat rate |
| Ops | Product availability, delivery zones, support owner |
| Separation | No accidental Fig & Bloom positioning leakage |

## Value Bundle Builder

For each bundle:

1. Define customer job and price point.
2. List included items and source.
3. Calculate contribution margin.
4. Define fulfilment process and substitutions.
5. Check whether the offer trains customers to wait for discounts.
6. Define stop-loss and review date.

Do not launch a value bundle without a margin table.

## CAC Payback Tracker

Track weekly:

- spend by channel;
- new customers;
- first-order CAC;
- AOV;
- contribution margin dollars;
- expected repeat value;
- payback period;
- creative winner/loser notes;
- action: scale, hold, cut, or retest.

Keep Bower metrics separate from Fig & Bloom. Blended reporting can be shown only after separated figures.

## Escalation

Escalate to the CEO for brand-positioning changes, spend above approved limits, cross-brand customer promises, or material margin risk.
